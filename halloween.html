Halloween Halloween is a popular and widely celebrated holiday that occurs annually on October 31. It has its roots in ancient Celtic and Christian traditions and has evolved over the centuries into a festive occasion marked by various customs and activities.The Celtic festival of Samhain, which marked the end of the harvest season and the beginning of winter, is often considered the precursor to Halloween.

CostumesOne of the most well-known Halloween traditions is dressing up in costumes. This practice has its roots in the ancient Celtic belief of disguising oneself to avoid recognition by spirits. Trick-or-treating became popular in the United States in the early 20th century. Children go door-to-door, saying "trick or treat" and receiving candies or other treats from neighbors.
