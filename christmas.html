Christmas Christmas is an annual festival celebrated by millions of people around the world, marking the birth of Jesus Christ. Observed on December 25th each year, Christmas is a time for religious observance and a cultural celebration, bringing people together to share joy, goodwill, and festivities.Christmas is primarily a Christian holiday that commemorates the birth of Jesus Christ in Bethlehem. The story is central to Christian beliefs and is narrated in the Bible's New Testament, particularly in the Gospels of Matthew and Luke.

Cultural TraditionBeyond its religious roots, Christmas has become a global cultural phenomenon with various traditions. These include decorating Christmas trees, hanging wreaths, exchanging gifts, and participating in festive meals. In many cultures, there's also a strong emphasis on spending time with family and friends.
