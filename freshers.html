Freshers In educational contexts, particularly in universities and colleges, "freshers" refer to new students who have recently enrolled in an institution for their undergraduate or postgraduate studies. The term is commonly used in the United Kingdom and some other countries. The period when freshers first arrive on campus is often called "Freshers' Week" or "Welcome Week."

Social EventsFreshers have the opportunity to participate in social events and activities designed to facilitate interactions with fellow students. These may include welcome parties, social mixers, and recreational events.
